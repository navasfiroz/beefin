What makes Beefin Burger different from other burger joints?

At Beefin Burger, we serve only authentic beef smash burgers made with 100% fresh beef patties — never frozen. Every burger is cooked to order, ensuring the perfect sear, juiciness, and flavor that sets us apart from typical fast-food burgers.

Do you use frozen meat for your beef patties?

Never. Our patties are prepared daily using fresh beef sourced with care. We believe in serving burgers the way they should be — freshly smashed, freshly seared, and full of flavor.

Where is Beefin Burger located?

Right now, Beefin Burger is exclusively available in Kayamkulam, Kerala, India. We’re excited to be building from our hometown roots and have plans to expand to more cities and even globally soon.

What kind of burgers can I order at Beefin Burger?

We specialize in classic American-style smash burgers made only with beef. Our menu focuses on quality over quantity, delivering the juiciest beef burgers with perfectly caramelized edges and premium toppings.

Is Beefin Burger planning to expand outside Kayamkulam?

Yes. While our first kitchen is proudly based in Kayamkulam, Kerala, our vision is to bring the Beefin Burger experience worldwide. Stay tuned as we announce new locations in the near future.
